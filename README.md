# The Cookbook 🍳

**A shared library of everything the community builds on [The Cookout](https://thecookout.dev).**

---

## What is this?

The Cookbook is the community's pantry.

When someone builds something on The Cookout — an agent, a prompt, a workflow, a template — and decides to share it, it ends up here. Free to browse, free to fork, free to make better.

This isn't a curated collection maintained by a small team. It's a living record of what real people built when they stopped waiting to feel ready and just started cooking.

---

## What's in here

Everything members create and choose to share:

- **Agents** — AI agents built for real problems, real workflows, real use cases
- **Prompts** — tested, community-validated prompts that actually work
- **Workflows** — step-by-step sequences people use to get things done with AI
- **Templates** — starting points so nobody has to build from zero

If someone built it on The Cookout and wanted others to have it, it's here.

---

## Who this is for

**If you're on The Cookout** — this is your shared kitchen. Browse what the community has made, fork anything that fits your needs, and share your own work when you're ready.

**If you found this on GitHub** — welcome. You don't need a platform account to explore, learn from, or contribute to what's here. The Cookout is a community for underrepresented builders who are figuring out AI by doing it. If that sounds like you, you're already one of us.

**If you want to contribute** — read the contributing guide below. PRs are open and every skill level is welcome.

---

## How to use something from here

1. Browse the folders or search for what you need
2. Fork the repo or copy what's useful
3. Remix it, adapt it, make it yours
4. If you improve it, consider sharing your version back

There's no wrong way to use this. Take what helps.

---

## How to contribute

The Cookbook grows when people add to it. Here's how:

1. Fork this repo
2. Add your agent, prompt, workflow, or template in the right folder
3. Follow the format in `CONTRIBUTING.md` — it's short, we promise
4. Open a pull request with a brief description of what you built and what it does

A maintainer will review it. We're not looking for perfection — we're looking for things that are honest, useful, and something you'd actually want someone else to have.

---

## A note on quality

Nothing here has to be polished. It just has to be real.

The Cookout was built on the idea that domain knowledge matters as much as technical skill — that the person who knows the problem deeply has just as much to contribute as the person who knows the code. The Cookbook carries that same belief. A prompt written by a teacher who figured out how to use AI in their classroom is just as valuable as one written by an engineer.

If you built it and it worked, it belongs here.

---

## About The Cookout

The Cookout is a potluck-style AI builder community for underrepresented builders — people who have the knowledge, the ideas, and the drive, but haven't always had the seat at the table.

It's built and maintained by [The Basics Central](https://thebasicscentral.org), a nonprofit focused on making technical tools and knowledge accessible to the people who need them most.

The Cookbook is the public-facing piece of that. Everything else lives at [thecookout.dev](https://thecookout.dev).
